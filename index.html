About Chitwan

Chitwan is known for its rich biodiversity and is home to the famous Chitwan National Park. Visitors can experience unique wildlife like the Bengal tiger, one-horned rhinos, and an abundance of bird species. Itâ€™s also a cultural hub where you can learn about the Tharu culture and enjoy traditional hospitality.

Things to Do

Elephant Safari: Take an elephant safari through Chitwan National Park and witness rhinos, deer, and other exotic wildlife up close.

Canoe Ride: Enjoy a serene canoe ride along the Rapti River and catch glimpses of crocodiles, exotic birds, and lush landscapes.

Accommodation

Hotel Jungle Crown - Located near the national park, offering comfortable rooms with jungle views and close access to safari tours.

Tharu Village Resort - A traditional Tharu-style stay with authentic cultural experiences and homely hospitality.
